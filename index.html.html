About / vision

A creator-led future for Himalayan travel.

TrippyVibeZ exists to make travel feel human again — local, story-driven and rooted in the energy of creators and communities.

Our story

We believe the next generation of travel should feel more personal than packaged. Instead of generic itineraries, TrippyVibeZ connects travelers with creators who can shape real experiences around culture, vibe and storytelling.

Why creator-led travel

Creators already bring people together online through perspective, trust and identity. Travel becomes more powerful when that connection moves into the real world.

Focus on Himalayan culture

The Himalayas hold layers of beauty, silence, tradition, art and community. We want every route to respect that depth and share it responsibly.

Future vision

A premium platform where creators host curated journeys, travelers discover places through stories and local communities benefit from better-designed tourism.
